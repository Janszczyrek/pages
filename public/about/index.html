Hello!#

I’m Janusz, and I’m currently studying Applied Computer Science master program at Jagiellonian University. I’ve always been interested in a variety of technology-related subjects, always learning about how various systems operate and how they might be enhanced. My Main Interests Although computer science is a broad field, I’ve discovered that I’m especially interested in:

  • Developing scalable, user-friendly web applications to address practical issues
  • Understanding the infrastructure that links our digital world through computer networks; staying up to date on new attack vectors; delving into reverse engineering techniques; and following OWASP guidelines in order to construct more secure systems
  • CI/CD processes - Streamlining development workflows by automating deployment pipelines
  • Infrastructure as Code: Declarative management of infrastructure using Terraform and OpenTofu

Always Learning#

What excites me most about this field is its dynamic nature. Technology evolves rapidly, and there’s always something new to discover. Whether it’s a emerging framework, a novel approach to system architecture, or an innovative DevOps practice, I’m always eager to expand my knowledge and skill set. Beyond my core interests, I enjoy experimenting with homelab projects and exploring cloud-native technologies. This hands-on approach helps me bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.
